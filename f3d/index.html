F3D

Festung 3D — a first-person maze inspired by the legendary early-'90s shooter that defined the genre, rebuilt entirely with MapLibre GL's 3D extrusion engine and free camera. No raycaster — every wall is a real georeferenced building.

Arrow keys (or WASD/ZQSD) to move, ← → or the mouse to turn.
